Ubuntu LTD — Email signature

Three signature variants pre-filled with Jonathan Okubay as the demo name. To use your own, replace the highlighted text fields. The signatures use inline styles only — Gmail and Outlook will preserve them when you paste.

How to install in Gmail:
  1. Below, find the variant you want (Full, Short, or OOO)
  2. Click anywhere inside the white panel for that variant
  3. Press Cmd+A (Mac) or Ctrl+A (Windows) to select everything inside the panel
  4. Press Cmd+C / Ctrl+C to copy
  5. Open Gmail → ⚙️ Settings → See all settings → General tab → scroll to "Signature"
  6. Click Create new, name it "Ubuntu LTD", paste in the signature box
  7. Scroll down and click Save Changes

How to change the name for your own signature

Open this file (email-signature.html) in any text editor and replace:
  • Jonathan Okubay → your name
  • Managing Director → your title
  • jonathan@ubuntultd.com → your email
  • +1 416 555 0140 → your phone
Then reload the file in a browser and follow the copy-paste steps above.

Brand rules to honour

Do
  • Keep it under four lines for the full variant
  • Use canonical domain: ubuntultd.com
  • Full org name on first mention: Ubuntu Land Trust & Developments
Don’t
  • No images (logos, photos, social icons)
  • No “Sent from my iPhone”
  • No motivational quotes
  • No rotating banner / promo lines
  • No pronouns inside the signature block
  • Never the .ca domain — the canonical domain is .com
