01 · Where the sites sit

Ten cities. One corridor.

Every site in the pipeline sits within the southern Ontario development corridor — concentrated in the Greater Toronto Area, Hamilton, and Simcoe County. National expansion follows the same playbook into Quebec, Alberta, and British Columbia in subsequent waves.

05 · What we publish

Site list, public. Deal terms, private.

Every site above — address, region, typology, unit count, binding-instrument status — is public information. The fact of the pipeline is the credibility.

Deal-level financials, landowner identities, operator pairings, and project pro formas sit behind partner-specific deal rooms. Funders, lenders, and prospective operators receive that detail once a confidentiality framework is in place.

If you’d like deeper diligence on a specific site or cohort, write to info@ubuntultd.com.
