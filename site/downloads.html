03 · Usage notes

How to use these.

Internal team

Use any template directly. Modify content but not formatting, unless you have a specific reason to. The point is consistency — recipients should recognize an Ubuntu LTD document immediately.

Approved partners and contractors

Partner agencies, designers, lawyers, and contractors producing work on Ubuntu LTD’s behalf may use these templates directly. For substantive deviations from the brand book, confirm with Jonathan Okubay (Managing Director) before publication.

Fonts

Templates default to Georgia (serif) and Consolas (mono) for universal rendering on any computer. For the full brand experience, install the actual brand fonts free from Google Fonts: Newsreader and JetBrains Mono. Documents will automatically render in the brand fonts once installed.

Updates

Templates and the AI skill are versioned in the GitHub repository. When numbers change (new sites, refreshed pipeline figures, new team members), the updates flow to this page. If you maintain a local copy of the AI skill, refresh it quarterly.
