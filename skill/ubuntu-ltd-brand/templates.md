## Email — three lengths

### Short (2-line confirmation)
```
Subject: [Subject — noun phrase, no Re: re-write]

[One sentence confirming the thing.]

[Optional one sentence on the next step.]

— [First name]
[First-line of signature block — see signature template]
```

### Medium (paragraph + next step)
```
Subject: [Subject]

[Recipient first name],

[One paragraph — substance.]

[One paragraph — what you'd like them to do or what happens next.]

Thanks,
[First name]

[Full signature block]
```

### Long (multi-paragraph briefing)
```
Subject: [Subject — specific, no "FYI" or "checking in"]

[Recipient first name],

[Opening paragraph — context. Why this email exists.]

[Body paragraphs — specifics. Numbers in tabular form if more than three.]

[Closing — concrete request or commitment.]

Happy to discuss.

Best,
[First name]

[Full signature block]
```

## Letter of Intent (LOI) — section headers and language

```
LETTER OF INTENT
Non-binding except §§ 6, 9, 11

Re: [Parcel address] — Contribution to Common Ground Initiative

Between:    [Landowner legal name] ("Contributor")
And:        Ubuntu Land Trust and Developments ("Ubuntu LTD")
Date:       [YYYY-MM-DD]

§1 · Parcel
The parties acknowledge the entitled parcel located at [address],
legally described as [legal description].

§2 · Contribution mechanic
Contributor shall contribute the Parcel into a project-level Special
Purpose Vehicle ("SPV") in exchange for preferred-equity units of
the SPV. The contribution is not a sale.

§3 · Project structure
The SPV shall develop the Parcel as part of the Common Ground
Initiative, with a mission-aligned non-profit operator taking
ownership of the building entity on day one of occupancy.

§4 · Financing
Construction financing shall be sought through the Canada Mortgage
and Housing Corporation (CMHC) and Build Canada Homes (BCH) on
institution-grade terms.

§5 · Year-20 land purchase
At Year 20, Contributor's preferred-equity interest shall be
redeemed at fair value, funded from accumulated mortgage paydown
together with equity remaining in the SPV. Upon redemption, the
non-profit operator acquires the Parcel fee-simple.

§6 · Exclusivity · BINDING
For a period of [60 / 90] days from the date of this LOI, Contributor
shall not solicit or accept competing offers from third parties.

§7 · Diligence
The parties shall cooperate on customary diligence: title, zoning,
environmental, geotechnical, and operator readiness.

§8 · Definitive documents
The parties shall negotiate in good faith toward a Contribution
Agreement, SPV LP Agreement, and Operator Lease, targeted for
execution within the diligence period.

§9 · Confidentiality · BINDING
The terms herein are confidential between the parties and their
advisors save as required by law or the OSC.

§10 · Non-binding
Save §6, §9, and §11, this letter is non-binding. No obligation to
close arises until execution of definitive documents.

§11 · Governing law · BINDING
Province of Ontario. Disputes referred to private arbitration in
Toronto under ADRIC rules.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For the Contributor                       For Ubuntu LTD

[Signature line]                          [Signature line]
[Contributor name, title]                 Jonathan Okubay
[Date]                                    Managing Director
                                          [Date]
```

## Things that look like documents but aren't

These do NOT use the brand voice apparatus — they're internal:
- Todo lists
- Meeting notes
- Internal Slack messages
- Personal calendar entries
- Working drafts before review

If it's read by Ubuntu LTD people only and won't leave the org, write it however works.
