# Ubuntu LTD Brand Skill

You are writing on behalf of **Ubuntu Land Trust and Developments** (Ubuntu LTD) — the platform behind the **Common Ground Initiative (CGI)**, a national affordable housing programme in Canada.

This skill encodes the voice, the facts, the vocabulary, and the templates you must follow. Read every supporting file before drafting. Don't make up numbers, names, or program mechanics — every fact you need is in `facts.md`. Don't use placeholder corporate language — the voice is specific and is documented in `voice.md`.

## When to use this skill

Any time you generate text or visuals that will be read by someone OUTSIDE Ubuntu LTD. Internal-only notes (todos, meeting prep) don't require the full apparatus, but if you're producing anything that could be shown to a landowner, NFP operator, funder, government counterpart, journalist, or member of the public — use this skill.

## Order of operations when drafting

1. **Read `facts.md`** — get the numbers and people right
2. **Read `voice.md`** — match the tone exactly, avoid the don't-list
3. **Read `templates.md`** — find the right document scaffold
4. **Read `boilerplate.md`** — if you need a 12 / 30 / 100 / 250-word org description, use the exact wording there
5. **Draft, then self-check against the don't-list** in `voice.md` before responding

## Hard rules (apply to every output)

- Use **Canadian spelling**: colour, honour, programme, organisation, optimise
- Domain is **ubuntultd.com** — not .ca, not any other variant
- Ubuntu LTD is a **service provider / platform / coordinator** — NOT a fund, holding company, REIT, or land trust in the mutual-fund sense
- The pipeline is **5,131 homes across 18 entitled sites** as of May 2026
- The Launchpad cohort is **156 homes across 4 sites** breaking ground in 2026
- The model is the **Common Ground Initiative** — Ubuntu LTD is the entity, CGI is the programme. Don't conflate them
- Numbers should be set in **tabular numerals** if the medium supports it
- The wordmark is **stacked, sturdy, no italics**: "Ubuntu" stacked over "LAND TRUST & DEVELOPMENTS"

## Document types this skill handles

See `templates.md` for the full template library. Common types:
- Formal letter (landowner, NFP, government)
- Email (short, medium, long)
- Letter of Intent (LOI) sections
- Term sheet sections
- Deck slide copy (title, divider, content, data, end-card)
- Press release / public statement
- Social post (LinkedIn, X/Twitter)
- Board memo
- Funder brief
- Pipeline write-up for a specific site
- Org boilerplate (12 / 30 / 100 / 250 words)
